Life on Earth

Since I was born, I travel around the universe looking for partners to enjoy my ego trips. Recently, I come to Earth, and I found that weird creatures call humans.
It's very dificcult to make friends here. I'm really excited because this creatures are weird and so common at the same time, that make me feel like a stranger here.
Last night, I was walking and found this bizarre message. Seems like they have really doubts about us, other creatures who live in galaxy.
It's not just ignorant, it's extraordinary because it's so inocent .

Before that, I realize that humans not just think they are unique in galaxy, but also they think they are important to somebody besides themselves .
Yeah, I know, I get shocked too.
It's very contradictory by the way, because the most people actually don't care for others. Including sometimes their own family, or children.
Some basic values to survive get losted in this crazy little planet.
I learned about hipsters. They are a group that really really believe that are super cool and super diferent, but they also common too (?).
